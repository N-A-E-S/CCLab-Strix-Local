Pixelime

Scientific Name:

Pixelime

Discovered by / at:

Sean Hu, Year 2077

Family:

Animalia-Mollusca-Gastropoda-Slimea-Pixelislimidae

Habitat:

Wetland, riverside. Such creature are often found in the high humidity environment, such as rianforest in Kalimantan Island

Appearance:

Pixelime got pixel yet smooth skin. They have no legs/arms. Body color covers most of the skin aside from the eyes and mouth

Key Characteristics:

  • The creature mainly moves via jumping.
  • They need to keep eating to keep their lives longer.
  • The color and name of newly born kids depend on their parents.

What do we know so far about the creature?

Despite being asexual, they still require two Pixelime situated close enough to each other to give birth to the new Pixelime. These organisms can only reproduce once they reach a certain age.

How do we interact with this creature?

We deploy new origin creatures add foods or even directly control the individual behavior.
